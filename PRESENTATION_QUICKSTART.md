## üöÄ How to Generate PDF (Simple Method)

### The presentation should already be open in your browser!

**Steps:**
1. Press `Ctrl + P` (or `Cmd + P` on Mac)
2. Choose **"Save as PDF"** as the printer/destination
3. Set **Orientation** to **"Landscape"**
4. Set **Margins** to **"None"** or **"Minimal"**
5. Enable **"Background graphics"**
6. Click **"Save"**
7. Save as: `CareerForge-AI-Presentation.pdf`
